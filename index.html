We Focus on Respect and Discipline

Focus and Discipline

By encouraging and challenging students to be accountable and responsible for their actions, Maple Taekwondo helps students to reach personal levels of focus and self-discipline they can be proud of.

Sportsmanship

Maple Taekwondo’s focus on fostering competence in martial arts is balanced with supporting students in developing their emotional intelligence which leads to good sportsmanship. So it follows that students become humble in victory and graceful in defeat.

Self Defense

While promoting and teaching non-violent strategies for conflict resolution, Maple Taekwondo also teaches simple yet practical self-defence techniques.

Respect

Fostering Respect for Self and Respect for Others is an essential element of the Taekwondo curriculum. Students are taught in a respectful climate and are supported in respectful relationships between their masters, their fellow classmates and their parents.

Improved Health and Fitness

Maple Taekwondo provides fun and safe ways for students to practice cardio, core, and strength training and can help instil a life-long desire and discipline for maintaining healthy living.

Flexible Schedule

Maple Taekwondo offers a variety of classes to accommodate a variety of age and belt levels to ensure that students receive the quality of instruction that is appropriate.

Master Chang Lee
6th Dan Black Belt

Since the age of seven, Master Lee has passionately embraced the life-long practice of Taekwondo in balance with other Martial Arts. As a highly trained martial arts competitor, he has had many opportunities to develop his skills. Having competed in Taekwondo, Kung Fu, Boxing, and Kick-Boxing in both Korea and Canada at provincial and national levels, Master Lee has offered a great deal of experience to his students for over 25 years.

David Licthy
3rd Dan Black belt

When Maple Taekwondo opened in 2004, David was nine years old and keen to start training. By ten, he was hooked and began competing in tournaments across Ontario. As Master Lee empowers all of his students, David was encouraged to share his learning early on and continues to develop his skills while teaching Taekwondo. Having training and experience as a camp counsellor and program director in several York region programs, David brings a love of kids and a fresh energy to his work with students.

Why Us?

Taekwondo offers a range of experiences for individuals seeking to learn about and engage in the martial arts. While our instruction focuses on the acquisition and development of skills we are also aware that the opportunity to develop personal skills and a sense of belonging are important. Instruction is intentional in fostering individual and collaborative learning through critical thinking, problem-solving and the development of personal management skills. We purposely align our instruction with the tenants of the Ontario Ministry of Education’s goals in that we are committed to encouraging and supporting a balance between the physical, emotional and intellectual well-being of our students.
